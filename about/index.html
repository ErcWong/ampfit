Always Making Progress


What does it mean to you? It can apply to anything and everything, whether in training or in everyday life. One must always try to keep progressing, but know that the journey will be difficult. Progression will not be a single upward climb; there will be many ups and downs. Sometimes to keep moving forward, a couple of steps back are necessary. Learn to embrace every step along the way.

Mastering the basics is first and foremost if you want to be Always Making Progress. If you want to progress beyond the average, if you want to be great, you must make time to master the basics. At AMPFit, this is our priority. A strong fundamental base is crucial for injury prevention and safe and effective strength training. Practice hard and earn a solid base, then become the master of your body and mind; free to build, change and move however you see fit.
