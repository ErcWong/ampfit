You have a decent grasp of how to use a barbell and are interested in taking your skills further. Perhaps you are interested in lifting for sport.

Enter Olympic Weightlifting or Weightlifting as it is commonly referred, a sport, which has two contested lifts: the Snatch and the Clean and Jerk.

In Olympic Weightlifting 101, you will learn how to prepare your body, how to perform the lifts, and Olympic Weightlifting variations and terminology.
