The AMP: Lifting Principles package is a 4-week package, which includes:

  • 4 Mandatory Private Training sessions • Scheduled weekly at your (or you and your friends) convenience.

  • 8 Optional Group Training sessions • Each week you will have 2 drop-in sessions during the scheduled group training times. Please check the Schedule for weekday training times. If your instructor feels that you do not adequately understand the concepts and movements taught in the 4 weeks, you may be (temporarily) denied access to AMPFit Group Training. Depending on the factors involved, we may require you to repeat AMP: Lifting Principles or sign up for some Private Sessions.
