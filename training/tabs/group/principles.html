AMP Lifting Principles

  • Interested in joining AMPFit Group Training?
  • Interested in lifting but aren't sure how to go about it?
  • Want to learn how to move better and get stronger?
  • Are you interested in effective ways to get lean and defined?

If you've answered yes to any of the above, then AMP: Lifting Principles is the place to start!

AMP: Lifting Principles is a required pre-requisite to attend AMPFit Group Training. You may choose to complete either AMP: Lifting Principles or Private AMP: Lifting Principles.

What you will learn:

  • Deadlift
  • Squat
  • Get-up
  • Push-up
  • TRX Row
  • Kettlebell Swing

The AMP Lifting Principles package includes 12 classes at AMPFit. It is a 4 week program which includes 3 training sessions per week, 1 Lifting Principles class every Saturday and includes 2 Group Training sessions during weekdays. Please check the Schedule for Group Training class times.

This is a class for those looking to refine their basic movement and lifting abilities. We will give you the necessary tools and teach you the principles to help you reach your individual goals. If you were previously interested in AMPFit classes, but afraid of committing to them, this is the class for you!
