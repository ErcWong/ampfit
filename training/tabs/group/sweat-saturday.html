AMP: Sweat Saturdays


Once you’ve gotten into the groove of things at AMP Group Training, you will have learned a variety of skills and exercises. Sweat Saturday is the day to put your training to the test. It’s also a great time to train with your fellow classmates and perhaps meet some new ones.

You must be approved and prepared before joining Sweat Saturdays. If one of your main goals is to attend, prioritize staying consistent in AMP Group Training. When you are ready, you will be informed.

Sweat Saturday is not for everyone. If Sweat Saturday does not fit your individual goals, it is highly recommended that you do not participate. Sweat Saturday is included with your AMP Group Training registration. It is held every Saturday starting at 1:30pm sharp, the gym is open beforehand so you may properly prepare and warm–up. Please arrive a minimum of 15 minutes prior to start time to get organized.
