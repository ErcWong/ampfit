AMP: Sweat Saturdays


Once you’ve gotten into the groove of things at AMPFit Group Training, you will have learned a variety of skills and exercises. Sweat Saturday is the day to put your training to the test. It’s also a great time to train with your fellow classmates and perhaps meet some new ones.

You must be approved and prepared before joining Sweat Saturdays. If one of your main goals is to attend, prioritize staying consistent in AMPFit Group Training. When you are ready, you will be informed and granted access to join the group. Sweat Saturday is not for everyone. If Sweat Saturday does not fit your individual goals, we do not recommend that you participate.

Sweat Saturday is included with your Group Training registration. Please arrive a minimum of 15 minutes prior to start time so you may properly warm–up, prepare and get organized.
