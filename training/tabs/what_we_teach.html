Movement

AMPFit strongly believes in the importance of practicing quality movement. We teach our students to focus on quality in practice to improve their basic movement patterns. Practicing with mindfulness provides our students with the strong foundation necessary to progress safely and effectively. This is the first step in Always Making Progress.

Strength

If you have a goal of getting stronger, AMPFit is a great place to start. You will learn principles that will aid you in your journey of getting stronger. Utilizing these principles, you will be able to apply them to variety of tools, which include but are not limited to: your own bodyweight, the kettlebell, and the barbell. Based on your individual goals and preferences, you may choose the training tools you use.

Bodyweight

What is strength without the ability to move your body freely and comfortably? It starts with something as simple as a plank. The next step, maybe you complete your first push-up. Then out of nowhere, your first chin-up. The possibilities are endless, use your imagination and set the goal. AMPFit is here to help you on the journey.

Kettlebell

At AMPFit, we utilize the kettlebell as the main introductory tool to teach the basics of movement and lifting. We teach StrongFirst or 'hard-style' kettlebell training which can be used to develop a great base of movement, power, and endurance. If you have a goal of leaning out, slimming down or just want a quick and effective workout, give the kettlebell a try!

View more

Barbell

The barbell is an amazing tool for building strength and muscle mass. The barbell will aid in building a strong foundation of strength. When you first start, the main barbell movements that we teach are: the Deadlift, the Squat and the Bench Press. As you progress, there are many more variations and exercises to learn.

Weightlifting

When we say Weightlifting, we are referring to the sport of Olympic Weightlifting. The sport of Olympic Weightlifting consists of two lifts: the 'Snatch' and the 'Clean and Jerk'. AMPFit is currently the only BCWA (British Columbia Weightlifting Association) Club in Richmond, BC.
