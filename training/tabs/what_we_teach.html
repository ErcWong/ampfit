Movement

AMPFit strongly believes in the importance of quality movement.

Kettlebell

At AMPFit, we utilize the kettlebell as the introductory tool to teach the basics of movement and lifting.

We teach StrongFirst or “hard-style” kettlebell training which is used to develop a great base of strength, power, and endurance.

If you have a goal of leaning out, slimming down or just want a quick and effective training session, give the kettlebell a try!

Barbell

The barbell is a great tool for building strength and muscle mass.

As always, it starts with the basics. AMPFit teaches these four basic barbell lifts to start: the Military Press, the Squat, the Bench Press and the Deadlift.

Weightlifting

When we say Weightlifting, we are referring to the sport of Olympic Weightlifting. The sport of Olympic Weightlifting consists of two lifts: the ‘Snatch’ and the ‘Clean and Jerk’.

AMPFit is currently the only BCWA (British Columbia Weightlifting Association) Club in Richmond, BC.
