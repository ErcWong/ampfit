Personal Training Policies:

If you have any questions regarding these policies, do not hesitate to ask.

- Payment: Payment must be made in advance before the start of your first session.

- Tardiness: All clients and trainers are encouraged to be prompt. If client is late, this time will be deducted from the session; contrarily, if a trainer is late, the amount of time will be added for an extended session. Please be advised that trainers are required to wait 15 minutes for a scheduled client, after which time the session is subject to cancellation and clients will be charged for a full session.

- Cancellations: In order to cancel or reschedule a session, please contact AMP at least 24 hours in advance of the scheduled appointment or you will be charged for that session. Similarly, if AMP does not contact you atleast 24 hours in advance to cancel or reschedule an appointment you will receive a complimentary session. If a client does not show up for a scheduled session, he/she will be charged for that session.

- Refunds: Please be sure that our services match your needs before committing through payment. If you are fully committed to your AMP training program and are still not satisfied with the services provided. AMP will be happy to provide you with a refund*.
